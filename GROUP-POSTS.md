# Daily Tester Group Posts

Ready-to-paste posts for `toss007@googlegroups.com` — one per day, matching each build in [ROADMAP.md](ROADMAP.md). Post after the day's build goes live on the closed track (Play review usually clears within a few hours).

Every post follows the same recipe Google wants to see: what changed, ask testers to **update + open the app**, and ask **one specific feedback question** so replies are easy.
